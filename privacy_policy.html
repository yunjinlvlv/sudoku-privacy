Privacy Policy — Sudoku Infinity

Last updated: June 25, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Sudoku Infinity app ("App") developed by Oh Yongjae ("Developer"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information through third-party services:

  • Advertising ID — Used by Google AdMob to serve personalized advertisements
  • Usage Data — Game events (game start, completion, difficulty, duration) collected via Firebase Analytics to improve the app experience
  • Crash Reports — Collected via Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix bugs

The App does not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information directly.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics — Analytics and crash reporting
  • Google Play Billing — In-app purchases

3. Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Ads may be personalized based on your interests using the Advertising ID. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

The App complies with the IAB Transparency and Consent Framework (TCF) and Google's EU User Consent Policy via the Google UMP SDK.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases ("Remove Ads", "Premium Themes", "Premium Bundle") processed by Google Play. Payment information is handled solely by Google Play and is not accessible to the Developer.

5. Data Retention

Game progress (board state, statistics) is stored locally on your device only. It is not transmitted to external servers. You can delete this data by uninstalling the App.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is rated for Everyone and contains only gameplay data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
